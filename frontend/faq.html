Frequently Asked Questions

What is Plant Simulation?
Plant Simulation is a tool used to simulate the growth and behavior of different plants. It helps users understand various aspects of plant life and provides insights into plant-related questions.
How do I search for plants?
To search for plants, simply type the name of the plant in the search bar and click "Enter". The system will provide you with relevant information about the plant.
Can I save my plant searches?
Yes, the plant names you search are stored temporarily. This allows you to view them again if you navigate back to the search results page.
