What plant is right for you?

Source: Liu, Y., Xu, X., Dimitrov, D. et al. An updated floristic map of the world. Nat Commun 14, 2990 (2023).

When deciding what plant is right for you, there are several factors to keep in mind.

The most important part is where you are located in the world - your availability of plants will heavily depend on what plants are native to your region. We encourage you to primarily focus on growing native plants, as they have a better likelihood of surviving.
